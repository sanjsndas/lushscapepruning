Cookies Policy

Welcome to LushScape Pruning’s Cookies Policy. This page provides comprehensive information on how cookies and similar technologies are utilized on our website and in our services. We believe in full transparency and wish you to understand how your online experience is enhanced and managed through cookies.

Contact-Related Topics Covered in This Cookies Policy

1. Website Contact Form Cookie Usage

Our website uses cookies to temporarily store information entered in the contact form. This helps save your input in case of accidental page refresh or navigating away and back. This way, your data isn't lost and you can continue filling without starting over.

These cookies do not transmit your personal data externally and are erased once the form is submitted or the session ends. They improve form usability and reduce frustration.

2. Cookies for Returning Visitors Contact Preferences

When you choose your preferred contact method, such as phone, email, or WhatsApp, cookies store this choice to personalize your future interactions. This allows the website to streamline communications and offers tailored on-site messages.

This personalization respects your preferences by not showing irrelevant contact options or prompts, thus enhancing user satisfaction.

3. Cookies Supporting Real-Time Chat Availability

Our live chat feature uses cookies to manage chat sessions and remember active chats. This ensures continuous communication without session loss even if you navigate multiple pages during the conversation.

Cookies also help display your chat availability status and queue position ensuring efficient support.

4. Enhancing Phone Support Experience With Cookies

Cookies may be used to recognize your device and faster connect you with your previous phone support representatives if you re-contact. This helps provide a consistent service experience without repeating information.

This feature is opt-in and only used to improve connection continuity.

5. Cookies Tracking Contact Form Submission Success

Cookies store temporary flags after successful submission of contact or feedback forms to prevent duplicate submissions and to provide relevant success messages upon reload or return visits.

This also assists us in tracking form usage and improving form design.

6. Cookies For Phone Call Tracking (Analytics)

To measure the effectiveness of phone contact campaigns or website call-to-action buttons, we utilize cookies that collect anonymized call event data. This helps optimize communication channels and improve customer service availability.

All tracking is compliant with data protection laws and does not collect personal call content.

7. Social Media Contact Integration Cookies

Cookies facilitate the use of embedded social media contact tools such as Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp chat buttons, or Instagram direct message links. They allow session management and facilitate seamless messaging between you and our social media profiles.

These cookies may be set by third parties, controlled under their policies, and aim to enhance convenience.

8. Cookies Saving Newsletter Subscription Contact Info

When subscribing to our newsletter, cookies store your subscription status and contact preferences to offer you personalized content and avoid redundant subscription requests, improving your user experience.

We use this data solely for email subscription management and communications.

9. Cookies Supporting Voice Contact Features

Some advanced contact methods like voice or video calls integrated into our site use cookies to manage session authentication, call duration, and quality optimization, ensuring smooth communication during the interaction.

Cookies used here do not store content but facilitate proper connection handling.

10. Multi-Channel Contact Cookie Synchronization

Cookies allow synchronizing your contact interaction preferences across multiple devices and browsers to maintain consistent user experience and avoid repetitive request for contact information or options.

This enables seamless switching between devices during communication with us.

11. Cookies Managing Callback Requests

Cookies store your preferences and request details for callbacks so that your requests can be managed efficiently and our customer service can follow up accurately within the agreed timeframe.

12. Cookies Facilitating Online Appointment Scheduling

When scheduling consultations or visits through our online system, cookies help keep your session active and remember your selections, avoiding loss of data during navigation.

13. Cookies and SMS/Text Message Contact Integration
